Introduction

We cordially invite all alumni, teachers, and staff to celebrate the 30th anniversary of our school. This milestone marks three decades of dedication to education and community. Join us for a memorable event that reflects on our past, celebrates our present, and looks toward the future.

Event Details

  • Date: December 15, 2025
  • Time: 18:00 – 21:00
  • Location: Main Hall of the School
  • Program: Opening ceremony, speech from the principal, performances, dinner, and networking.

Invitation

In honor of our 30 years of history, we extend a heartfelt invitation to all former students and teachers to join us in this celebration. Your presence will make the event more special, and we look forward to sharing this significant moment with you.
